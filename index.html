Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and salon policies.

What safety measures do you have in place?
We follow strict sanitation protocols including tool sterilization between clients, fresh linens for each guest, and air purification systems. Our staff is fully vaccinated and we follow all local health guidelines.
How far in advance should I book an appointment?
We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for peak times (weekends, evenings) and for complex services like color corrections. You can join our waitlist for last-minute cancellations.
Do you offer refunds if I'm not happy with my service?
We stand by our work and will gladly make any adjustments within 7 days of your service at no additional charge. Please contact the salon within 48 hours to discuss your concerns.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations and no-shows will be charged 50% of the scheduled service value. Multiple missed appointments may require prepayment for future bookings.
Can I bring my children to my appointment?
For safety reasons, we ask that children not receiving services not be brought to the salon unless supervised by another adult. We offer children's haircut services on weekday mornings.
